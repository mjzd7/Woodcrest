Our Deluxe Rooms offer a luxurious and comfortable stay for guests who want to experience the ultimate in relaxation and style. With a spacious layout and elegant decor, the Deluxe Room is designed to provide the perfect retreat for couples or solo travelers seeking a peaceful getaway.

Each Deluxe Room features a plush king-size bed with premium linens, ensuring a restful night's sleep. Guests can also enjoy a comfortable sitting area with a sofa and armchair, perfect for reading a book or enjoying a cup of coffee while gazing at the stunning views of the lush gardens or the ocean.

The room is equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV with international channels, a minibar, and a coffee maker. The marble bathroom features a walk-in shower, plush towels, and luxury toiletries, providing guests with the ultimate in comfort and relaxation.

Our Deluxe Rooms are the perfect choice for those who want to indulge in a luxurious and comfortable stay at Breezie Hotel and Resort. With personalized service and world-class amenities, guests can be assured of a memorable stay that they will cherish for years to come.
